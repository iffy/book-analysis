to charles smith esq tunbridge wells and dated from london as far back as july dear smith--i have received yours your kindness almost overpowers me i wish nature had made such hearts as yours more common but i have lived three-and-twenty years in the world and have seen none like it at present believe me i have no need of your services being in cash again give me joy i have got rid of sir walter and miss they are gone back to kellynch and almost made me swear to visit them this summer but my first visit to kellynch will be with a surveyor to tell me how to bring it
