disappeared down the staircase while his companion muffling his features more closely than before in the folds of his mantle passed almost close to franz and descended to the arena by an outward himself that the two men whose conversation he had overheard were at a sufficient distance to prevent his encountering them in his descent in ten minutes after the strangers had departed franz was on the road to the piazza di spagna listening with studied indifference to the learned dissertation delivered by albert after the manner of pliny and calpurnius touching the iron-pointed nets used to prevent the ferocious beasts from springing on the spectators franz let him proceed without interruption and in fact did not hear what was said he longed to be alone and free to ponder over all that
