york and i think it was near the end of june before we saild there were then two of the packet-boats which had been long in port but were detained for the generals letters which were always to be ready to-morrow another packet arrivd she too was detaind and before we saild a fourth was expected ours was the first to be dispatchd as having been there longest passengers were engaged in all and some extremely impatient to be gone and the merchants uneasy about their letters and the orders they had given for insurance it being war time for fall
