his excellency general braddock having been pleased to empower me to contract for the hire of the same i hereby give notice that i shall attend for that purpose at lancaster from this day to next wednesday evening and at york from next thursday morning till friday evening where i shall be ready to agree for waggons and teams or single horses on the following terms viz that there shall be paid for each waggon with four good horses and a driver fifteen shillings per diem and for each able horse with a pack-saddle or other saddle and furniture two shillings
