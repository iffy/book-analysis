the fact himself--mcmaster i once purposd answering the abb and actually began the answer but on consideration that my writings contained a description of experiments which anyone might repeat and verify and if not to be verifid could not be defended or of observations offerd as conjectures and not delivered dogmatically therefore not laying me under any obligation to defend them and reflecting that a dispute between two persons writing in different languages might be lengthened greatly by mistranslations and thence misconceptions of one anothers meaning much of one of the abbs letters being founded on an error in the translation
