the fact himself--mcmaster i once purpos'd answering the abb and actually began the answer but on consideration that my writings contained a description of experiments which anyone might repeat and verify and if not to be verifi'd could not be defended or of observations offer'd as conjectures and not delivered dogmatically therefore not laying me under any obligation to defend them and reflecting that a dispute between two persons writing in different languages might be lengthened greatly by mistranslations and thence misconceptions of one another's meaning much of one of the abb's letters being founded on an error in the translation
