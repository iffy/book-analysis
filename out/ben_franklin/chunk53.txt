folger one of the first settlers of new england of whom honorable mention is made by cotton mather in his church history of that country entitled magnalia christi americana as a godly learned englishman if i remember the words rightly i have heard that he wrote sundry small occasional pieces but only one of them was printed which i saw now many years since it was written in in the home-spun verse of that time and people and addressed to those then concerned in the government there it was in favour of liberty of conscience and in behalf of the baptists quakers
