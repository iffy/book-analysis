erratum while i lodgd in little britain i made an acquaintance with one wilcox a bookseller whose shop was at the next door he had an immense collection of second-hand books circulating libraries were not then in use but we agreed that on certain reasonable terms which i have now forgotten i might take read and return any of his books this i esteemd a great advantage and i made as much use of it as i could my pamphlet by some means falling into the hands of one lyons a surgeon author of a book entitled the infallibility of human judgment
