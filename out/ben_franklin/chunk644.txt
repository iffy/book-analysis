the easiest while i was as afore mentiond detaind at new york i receivd all the accounts of the provisions etc that i had furnishd to braddock some of which accounts could not sooner be obtaind from the different persons i had employd to assist in the business i presented them to lord loudoun desiring to be paid the balance he causd them to be regularly examined by the proper officer who after comparing every article with its voucher certified them to be right and the balance due for which his lordship promisd to give me an order on the paymaster
