the easiest while i was as afore mention'd detain'd at new york i receiv'd all the accounts of the provisions etc that i had furnish'd to braddock some of which accounts could not sooner be obtain'd from the different persons i had employ'd to assist in the business i presented them to lord loudoun desiring to be paid the balance he caus'd them to be regularly examined by the proper officer who after comparing every article with its voucher certified them to be right and the balance due for which his lordship promis'd to give me an order on the paymaster
