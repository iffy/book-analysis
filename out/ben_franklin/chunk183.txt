thro some discontent with his wifes relations he purposed to leave her on their hands and never return again having taken leave of my friends and interchangd some promises with miss read i left philadelphia in the ship which anchord at newcastle the governor was there but when i went to his lodging the secretary came to me from him with the civillest message in the world that he could not then see me being engaged in business of the utmost importance but should send the letters to me on board wished me heartily a good voyage and a speedy return etc
