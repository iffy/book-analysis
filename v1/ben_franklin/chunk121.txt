working dress my best clothes being to come round by sea i was dirty from my journey my pockets were stuff'd out with shirts and stockings and i knew no soul nor where to look for lodging i was fatigued with traveling rowing and want of rest i was very hungry and my whole stock of cash consisted of a dutch dollar and about a shilling in copper the latter i gave the people of the boat for my passage who at first refus'd it on account of my rowing but i insisted on their taking it a man being sometimes more
