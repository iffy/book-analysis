harry had failed one after the other and i should probably soon follow them and therefore i was forbidden the house and the daughter shut up whether this was a real change of sentiment or only artifice on a supposition of our being too far engaged in affection to retract and therefore that we should steal a marriage which would leave them at liberty to give or withhold what they pleas'd i know not but i suspected the latter resented it and went no more mrs godfrey brought me afterward some more favorable accounts of their disposition and would have drawn me
