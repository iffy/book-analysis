opportunities of obtaining news his paper was thought a better distributer of advertisements than mine and therefore had many more which was a profitable thing to him and a disadvantage to me for tho i did indeed receive and send papers by the post yet the publick opinion was otherwise for what i did send was by bribing the riders who took them privately bradford being unkind enough to forbid it which occasion'd some resentment on my part and i thought so meanly of him for it that when i afterward came into his situation i took care never to imitate it
