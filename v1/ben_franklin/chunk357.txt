or it so appears to me at present when another asserted something that i thought an error i deny'd myself the pleasure of contradicting him abruptly and of showing immediately some absurdity in his proposition and in answering i began by observing that in certain cases or circumstances his opinion would be right but in the present case there appear'd or seem'd to me some difference etc i soon found the advantage of this change in my manner the conversations i engag'd in went on more pleasantly the modest way in which i propos'd my opinions procur'd them a readier reception
